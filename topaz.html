History

One little known piece of American history is that, shortly following
the attacks on Pearl Harbour, Japanese Americans and Americans of
Japanese Descent were forced to leave their homes and occupy small
camps for extended and indefinite amounts of time. Come find out
about this chapter in American history by visiting us!

Topaz is only one of the many Japanese Internment Camps
that you can visit throughout the country
